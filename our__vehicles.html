Our Vehicles

At LUVTOP Driving School, we pride ourselves on using reliable, well-maintained vehicles that cater to different learning needs. Whether you're mastering automatic or manual transmission, our fleet ensures a safe and effective driving experience.

Toyota RAV4 1999

Our crimson Toyota RAV4 is perfect for mastering automatic transmission, providing smooth gear changes that allow learners to focus on traffic awareness and advanced driving techniques.

Audi 200 C3

The classic Audi 200 C3 is ideal for learning manual transmission, offering a straightforward gear-shifting experience that builds essential driving skills and confidence on the road.
